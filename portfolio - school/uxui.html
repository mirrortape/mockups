UX/UI Design

I offer various services related to UX/UI design. I have been studying best practices for UX Research for 3 years, and I have also been producing professional wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for the web since 2017. I have the resources and the knowledge to help streamline all aspects of your next big web project. Below you can learn about some of the key services I offer for UX/UI design.



UX Research

UX research is the study of target users and their requirements, to add realistic contexts and insights to design processes. I have practical experience researching target users to understand how to use gathered metrics to objectively improve the design of a website.


Wireframing

Wireframing is an important first step in planning UX/UI design for websites. I produce versatile and readable wireframes that help form a basis on which to start a new web design.


Prototyping

Prototyping is one of the last key pre-production tasks in web design. Creating a design prototype involves coding a rough copy web page based off of previously made wireframes & mockups. As one of my main focuses is theme development, I am exceptional at producing accurate web prototypes over tight deadlines.
