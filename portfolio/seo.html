SEO

Search Engine optimization, or 'SEO', is another critical practice in modern web development wherein various methods are used to increase relevant user traffic, search appearances, and other valuable metrics for websites. I have experience working with SEO from the ground up; metadata, SEO writing, market research, and analysis tools are all part of my inventory of methods to optimize websites to meet their business goals. Below are some of the key practices I use to get the most out of SEO.



Market Research

Understanding the user market is key to being successful when beginning SEO on a website. Through various public and private resources I gather the most relevant metrics to better understand return users and target audiences to improve and optimize websites towards certain objectives.


Metadata Implements

Metadata describes invisible tags used by search engines to gather data on a website. Researching and implementing the best meta tag keywords is one of my key practices when performing SEO for a website. Tag keywords are always changing with trends so I put effort into understanding the current keyword trends that provide meaningful results.


Keyword Research

Similar to the other key practices, keyword research involves researching and implementing not only meta tags, but also visible page content that is optimized for the best results. I have experience with both writing and editing content for SEO purposes.
